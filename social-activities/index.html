"Education Is Not A Privilege, It Is A Right"

I Dr. Kamal Kumar Kushwah being the President of the Vigyan Club at Aatma Nirbhar Bharat have been a part of a kind amount of social activities in works in my career. I taught at a few primary government school to the children in need of education. I participated in social activities such as clothes and stationery distribution with a couple of NGOs which also included Kaarvaan- an NGO by JEC students itself. I will also part of movements which demanded an equal rights of education for all the children in not the best condition free of cost and have done my best to support it. Recently last years childrens day was celebrated by a JEC family with the students of Government Primary School of Gokulpur here in Jabalpur. The celebration ended with distribution of pencil boxes to the students there. It was a very nice and kind opportunity which I was given a chance to be a part of. Also, in the past under Municipal Corporation of Jabalpur events have an organised and once such event called "Aasmano Se Aage" was organised and around 150 science students and a few students with geography major were presented with small experiences, which they enjoyed thoroughly and they were also given career advices which provided them with a fresh,new and enhanced guidance towards their future by well renowned personalities and scientists. I have been fortunate enough to be a part of distinguished events and activities aiming at society welfare.
