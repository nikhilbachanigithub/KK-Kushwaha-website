Biographical Sketch

He has completed his graduation in[B.Sc(Electronics),]then he has completed his post graduation in [M.Sc(physics)], M.Phil and then he has completed Ph.D.

Currently he is working as Associate Professor & Head of Department, Department of Applied Physics at Jabalpur Engineering College, Jabalpur, MP, India. Having expertise in areas of Material science, Analog & Digital Communication, Optoelectronic devices, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning have been resource person of Anveshika Vigyan Prasar Yojna initiated by IIT Kanpur. I have been holding key role in Atma Nirbhar Madhya pradesh, India and am a regular reviewer of various journals of international repute and has published many research paper in reputed journals.
